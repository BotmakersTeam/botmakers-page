About project

We build custom modular solution for chat-bots, based on Bot Framework and azure services. Our team's aim is to provide fast innovational solution for individual customer needs. We have integration with luis, Text Analytics, Spell Check, Translator, Azure Search and many other services to turn bot into smart multi-languages speaker, able to understand client's mood and intentions.



About Us

We are passionate about programming students from two different universities - Lodz University of Technology in Poland and National University of Kyiv Mohyla academy in Ukraine. We apply for Imagine Cup 2018 competition to demonstrate our solution for changing the industry of chat-bots making. We believe that complex and centralized system can decrease price and improve quality of chat-bots solutions.
